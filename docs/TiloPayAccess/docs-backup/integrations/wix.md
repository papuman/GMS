### Connecting Tilopay
- Go to Accept Payments in your site's dashboard. Tip: Check your business location at the top right. If incorrect, click the drop-down menu to change it.
- Click See More Payment Options.
- Click Connect next to Tilopay.

- Depending on whether you need to create a new Tilopay account or have an existing account, follow the instructions:

- Enter your KEY, Password and Username.
- Click Connect.

- Click Create Tilopay account.
- Register for a Tilopay merchant account to receive your credentials.
- Once your merchant account is approved, return to this window (repeat steps 1-4) and follow the steps under Have an account to connect your account.


### FAQs
- Go to Accept Payments in your site's dashboard.
- Click Manage next to Tilopay.
- Enable the toggle next to Credit / Debits Cards.
