## üöÄ Fast Upload Method (5-10 minutes per category)

### Method 1: Direct Upload via Odoo
1. Go to **Sales ‚Üí Products ‚Üí Products**
2. Click on product name
3. Click **Edit**
4. Click the image area
5. Google image search: "[product name exact]"
6. Right-click product image ‚Üí Save As
7. Upload to Odoo
8. Click **Save**

### Method 2: Bulk Download then Upload
1. Open Google Images in one tab
2. Open Odoo Products in another tab
3. Download 10-20 images at once
4. Upload them one by one to matching products
