## Design Philosophy

### Material Design Principles Applied

1. **Elevation**: Cards use shadows to show hierarchy
2. **Motion**: Smooth, purposeful animations (300ms)
3. **Color**: Bold, meaningful colors for status
4. **Typography**: Clear hierarchy with size/weight
5. **Iconography**: Consistent, recognizable symbols
6. **Layout**: Grid-based, responsive
7. **Feedback**: Immediate visual confirmation

### User-Centered Design

- **Minimize Clicks**: 3 clicks max for any task
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Most important actions largest
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Show details on demand
- **Error Prevention**: Clear status indicators
- **Forgiving**: Easy to undo/cancel
